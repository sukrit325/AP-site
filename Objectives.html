Objectives

  • To study the basic properties of semiconductor materials such as Silicon, Germanium, and Gallium Arsenide.
  • To compare the band gap energies of Silicon, Germanium, and Gallium Arsenide and understand their effect on electrical behavior.
  • To analyze carrier mobility in different semiconductor materials and study its influence on device speed and performance.
  • To compare the electrical conductivity of the selected semiconductor materials.
  • To study the temperature dependence of semiconductor materials and understand how temperature affects their conductivity and performance.
  • To perform simulation analysis (using Python or theoretical models) to visualize and compare material performance.
  • To understand the practical applications of each semiconductor material in electronic and communication devices.
  • To identify the advantages and limitations of each material for different engineering applications.
  • To develop analytical and research skills in semiconductor physics and material comparison.
  • To understand the importance of material selection in the design and performance of semiconductor devices.
